Delivery Information

Everything you need to know about shipping and delivery

⏱️

Delivery Timelines

We source quality products directly from trusted suppliers. Delivery times vary depending on the item.

  • Items in stock locally: 2 to 5 working days
  • Items sourced on request: 2 to 4 weeks

We will always communicate your expected delivery window before you pay.

💰

Delivery Fees

Delivery fees depend on your location and the size of your order. We will confirm the exact fee with you before finalizing your order.

We deliver nationwide across all 36 states in Nigeria, including major cities and towns.

📦

Order Tracking

Once your order is placed, you will receive updates at these stages:

  1. Order Confirmed
  2. Sourcing from Supplier
  3. In Transit to Nigeria
  4. Arrived Locally
  5. Out for Delivery
  6. Delivered

You can also reach out on WhatsApp anytime to check your order status.

🔄

Returns & Issues

If your item arrives damaged or is incorrect, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a return or replacement at no extra cost to you.

We take quality seriously and inspect items before dispatch. Your satisfaction is our priority.
