How It Works

Getting your quality goods is simple and straightforward

1

Browse & Select

Browse our collection of carefully sourced products across beauty, kitchen, electronics, and home essentials. Everything you see is available to order. Take your time and pick exactly what you want.

2

Place Your Order & Pay

Found what you want? Add to cart, check out, and pay securely using your card, bank transfer, or USSD via Paystack. Your payment is fully protected.

3

We Source Your Items

Once your order is confirmed and payment is received, we source your items directly from our trusted suppliers in China. Quality is checked at every stage.

4

Shipping & Delivery

Your order is shipped to Nigeria and delivered straight to your doorstep. You will receive updates at every stage so you always know where your order is.
