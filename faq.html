Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our shop, add items to your cart, and check out. Pay securely via Paystack using your card, bank transfer, or USSD.

How long will my order take?

Most orders arrive within 2 to 4 weeks. If an item is already in stock locally, it ships within 2 to 5 working days.

Is my payment safe?

Yes. All payments are processed securely through Paystack, a trusted payment platform used by thousands of businesses across Nigeria.

Can I return an item?

If an item arrives damaged or is not what you ordered, contact us within 48 hours of delivery and we will make it right. Due to our sourcing model, we do not accept returns for change of mind.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes, we deliver across all states in Nigeria. Delivery fees depend on your location and the size of your order.

The item I want is not on the site. Can you source it for me?

Yes! Send us a message on WhatsApp with details or a picture of what you are looking for, and we will let you know if we can get it and the price.

How do I track my order?

Visit our Track Order page and enter your Order ID to see your delivery status at any time.
