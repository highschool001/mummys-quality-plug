Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our shop, add items to your cart, and check out. Pay securely via Paystack using your card, bank transfer, or USSD. Once payment is confirmed, we start sourcing your order.

How long will my order take?

Most orders arrive within 2 to 4 weeks. If an item is already in stock locally, it ships within 2 to 5 working days. Each product page has an estimated timeline so you know what to expect before ordering.

Is my payment safe?

Yes. All payments are processed securely through Paystack, a trusted payment platform used by thousands of businesses across Nigeria. We do not store your card details.

Can I return an item?

If an item arrives damaged or is not what you ordered, contact us within 48 hours of delivery and we will make it right. Due to the nature of our sourcing model, we do not accept returns for change of mind. Please review product details carefully before ordering.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes, we deliver across all states in Nigeria. Delivery fees depend on your location and the size of your order. We will confirm the exact delivery fee before finalizing your order.

The item I want is not on the site. Can you source it for me?

Yes! We can help source specific items from China. Send us a message on WhatsApp with details or a picture of what you are looking for, and we will let you know if we can get it and the price.

Do I pay before or after delivery?

Payment is made upfront when you place your order. This allows us to source your items immediately and ensures your order is processed without delay.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you will receive updates at every stage: Order Confirmed, Sourcing, In Transit to Nigeria, Arrived Locally, Out for Delivery, and Delivered. You can also reach out on WhatsApp anytime for a status update.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept debit cards, bank transfers, and USSD payments through Paystack. All major Nigerian banks are supported.
